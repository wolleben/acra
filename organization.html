ACRA Mission Statement

"The American Collegiate Rowing Association (ACRA) is the governing body for the ACRA national championship regatta. We provide a high-quality championship regatta experience for collegiate club programs and all programs outside the NCAA/IRA structure. We determine the national champions (boat and team), as well as All-Academic & Athletic All-American honorees from ACRA programs. Through our diverse entries at the ACRA Championship, we promote the growth and improvement of collegiate rowing across the spectrum of our membership. We seek to combine the best of our sport’s unique past, present, and future to provide our member athletes the best possible overall experience."
